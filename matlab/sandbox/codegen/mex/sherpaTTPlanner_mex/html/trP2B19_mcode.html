1%TRP2B Generates the homogeneous transformation matrix between the body
 2%and pan coordinate frames. kC is a struct containing the kinematic
 3%constants of the leg. legNum indicates the number of the leg that is being
 4%considered.
 5%
 6%Inputs:
 7%-kC: Struct of kinematic constants of the Sherpa_TT leg
 8%-legNum: The leg number identification.
 9%Outputs:
10%-TP2B: The homogeneous transformation matrix that is used to transform
11%coordinates from the pan frame to the body frame.
12%
13%trP2B.m
14%author:    wreid
15%date:      20150214
16
17function TP2B = trP2B(kC,legNum)
18    TP2B = trDH(kC.legAngleOffset(legNum),kC.B2PZOffset,kC.B2PXOffset,0);
19end
